We make the world a fun place through games and other interactive entertainment.
We are the largest game-centric live streaming platform in China and a pioneer in the eSports value chain.<q>We operate our platform both on PC and mobile apps, through which users can enjoy immersive and interactive games and entertainment live streaming.
Game-centric live streaming revenue accounted for 13.8%, 24.1% and 28.0% of total live streaming revenue in 2016, 2017 and 2018 in China.<q>Revenues from China's game-centric live streaming market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 24.7% from 2018 to 2023, which is nearly twice the expected growth rate of the non-game-centric live streaming market.<q>Against the backdrop of eSports' booming popularity, China has a massive and growing gamer community that is seeking interactive and engaging entertainment through game live streaming.
With 110.1 million average MAUs on our PC platform and 49.1 million average MAUs on our mobile platform, we had 159.2 million average total MAUs during the first quarter of 2019, representing year-over-year growth of 25.7% from 126.7 million average total MAUs during the same period of 2018.<q>Our platform attracts a large number of highly loyal and engaged users.<q>As of December 31, 2016, 2017 and 2018 and March 31, 2019, we had 98.7 million, 182.1 million, 253.6 million and 280.9 million registered users, respectively.
Approximately 440 and 780 of our streamers had more than one million viewers during the first quarter of 2018 and 2019, respectively.<q>Our exclusive contract model with top streamers helps ensure a consistent supply of quality content, which is effectively supplemented by the talent agency model that captures a large group of promising and rising streamers.<q>Our platform brings together a deep pool of top streamers and provides a sustainable streamer development system.
As one of the first game-centric live streaming platforms to make the foray into eSports, we are strategically positioned to benefit from the proliferation of the eSports industry in China.<q>The eSports industry generates highly attractive content and helps to transform our platform into an engaged and vibrant community.<q>Our average total eSports MAUs were approximately 78.3 million and 125.3 million in the first quarter of 2018 and 2019, respectively.
We have built powerful technology infrastructure to help ensure a stable and optimized live streaming experience for our users.<q>The optimized user experience attracts a large number of users on our platform and enables us to collect and analyze vast amounts of behavioral data leveraging our big data analytics capabilities.<q>Investing in the user experience generates significant benefits for our platform.
Live streaming is our main monetization channel and generated 77.7%, 80.7%, 86.1%, 81.5% and 90.9% of our total net revenue in 2016, 2017, 2018 and for the three months ended March 31, 2018 and 2019 respectively.<q>Our live streaming revenue is primarily derived from the sales of a wide array of virtual gifts.<q>These channels effectively supplement each other and unleash future monetization potential.
